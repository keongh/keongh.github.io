About Me

I have been working in software development since graduating with my Master's in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Georgia Tech in May 2018. My first position was with Liberty Defense Technologies, an Atlanta-based startup using 3D radar imaging technology developed by researchers at MIT Lincoln Labs to design a physical security system capable of real-time automatic weapon detection. I primarily worked on the AI subsystem, building our automated detection system from the ground up with the help of two other engineers. I am currently looking for new opportunities in backend or fullstack development as well as data science/machine learning. I am comfortable with several programming languages including Java, Python, C, C++, C#, and JavaScript as well as several backend and frontend development frameworks. I love to learn new things, and I am very confident in my ability to do so quickly.
